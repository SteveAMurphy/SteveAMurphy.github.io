Life in Metaphor

The Connor Pass linking Killarney to Dingle. Not for the faint hearted, the road etiquette relies on drivers allowing several cars to pass at any limited straight section. In the old days, the coaches of tourists had an agreeement that they would all use the road as a one way system on the Ring of Kerry.
